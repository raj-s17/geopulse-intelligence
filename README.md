## 📌 About

GeoPulse Intelligence analyses four datasets — **Brent Oil**, **Gold**, **Silver**, and the **SWIFT Global Currency Tracker** — and links the 2025–2026 price and currency movements to real geopolitical events (the Iran–Hormuz crisis and BRICS de-dollarisation), all explained in plain language for a non-technical audience.

> *"Oil is the fear thermometer that spiked fastest; gold and silver are the slower structural story at record highs; the US dollar is still king, but the throne is quietly contested by the yuan."*

## 📊 Data & Methodology

| Dataset | Coverage | Rows |
|---|---|---|
| Brent Oil | Jan 1946 – Mar 2026 | 963 |
| Gold | Jan 1915 – Apr 2026 (111 yrs) | 1,336 |
| Silver | Jan 1915 – Apr 2026 (111 yrs) | 1,336 |
| SWIFT Currency Tracker | Dec 2025 – Mar 2026 | 330 |

All figures are computed directly from the datasets — trends, stats, SWIFT payment shares, and Pearson correlations (**Gold–Silver r = 0.95**, Brent–Gold 0.46, Brent–Silver 0.51). Event context comes from the course-supplied reports (Wall Street Ledger, *Critical New Context*, Reserve Storm), cross-checked with IMF COFER. Event↔price links rest on **timing (association, not proven causation)**.

## 🛠️ Built with

Vanilla **JavaScript** + **Chart.js** (via CDN) — no frameworks, no build step, no dependencies. The entire app is a single self-contained `index.html`, which is why it hosts on GitHub Pages for free.
